About Me

I am Patty Luminouz.

My passion in design is to understand people's behaviors and emotions, and project that insight into a meaningful and effective design. Becoming a UX designer allows me to be a great version of myself.

more about me

I was born and raised in Bangkok, Thailand, but really started learning about life and being independent in Tokyo, Japan. Now I am in San Francisco to follow my own American dream. I love to travel and to explore and experience different cultures. Together with their experiences, I believe that culture is a big influence on a person's behavior, way of thinking, and personality. With my experience and my culture I see myself as very intuitive, versatile, great at multi-tasking, and highly motivated. I am observant and a fast learner, which allows me to adapt into any situation, yet being myself
